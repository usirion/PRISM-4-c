Guidance for data collection:

  • Age: Newborn, 0 to 1 month; Infant ⋗1 month to 12 months; Child > 12 months to 144 months; Adolescent: > 144 months.
  • Temperature can be oral, rectal, blood or axillary.
  • Neurological status includes only patients with known or suspected acute CNS disease. Do not use if > 2 hours of sedation, paralysis or anesthesia. If you are using continuous sedation and/or paralysis, use the period without sedation, paralysis or anesthesia close to ICU admission.
  • Do not use heart rate value during crying or iatrogenic agitation
  • Non-reactive pupils must be > 3mm; do not use this criterion after iatrogenic dilation
  • Acid-base disturbance: use calculated bicarbonate only if total CO2 is not routinely measured. pH and PCO2 can be arterial, capillary or venous sample.
  • PaO2 only arterial measurement.
